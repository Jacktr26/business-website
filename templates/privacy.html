Privacy Policy

We’re Launchly Studios. This policy explains what we collect, why we collect it, and your rights under UK GDPR.

What we collect

  • Contact form: name, email, and message so we can reply.
  • Bookings & payments: we use Stripe to process payments. Card data never hits our servers.
  • Analytics: we may use privacy-friendly analytics to understand site usage. If we use cookies, you’ll be notified.

How we use data

We use your information to respond to enquiries, manage bookings, process payments, and improve our service.

Sharing

We don’t sell your data. We only share necessary info with service providers (e.g., Stripe) to deliver our services.

Retention

We keep enquiry records for up to 12 months and booking/payment records for up to 6 years (as required by UK law).

Your rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your data. Contact us at hello@launchlystudios.com.
